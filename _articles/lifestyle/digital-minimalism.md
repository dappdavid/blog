In a world where we are constantly bombarded with notifications, emails, and social media updates, minimalism is becoming more relevant than ever.

*   **Declutter your phone:** Remove apps you don't use.
*   **Turn off notifications:** Keep only the essential ones.
*   **Organize your files:** Keep your digital workspace clean.

By adopting a minimalist approach to our digital lives, we can reclaim our time and attention.
